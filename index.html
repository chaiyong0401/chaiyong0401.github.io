Publications

Hierarchical Reactive Grasping for Contact-Rich Manipulation via Topological Visuo-Tactile Learning

C. Mun, E. Sung, H. Lee, and J. Park

Accepted for publication at IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), 2026.

This work presents a hierarchical visuo-tactile learning framework for contact-rich robotic manipulation. The proposed method combines a low-frequency semantic planning policy with a high-frequency reactive action decoder, enabling robots to modulate grasp force in response to tactile contact changes such as incipient slip. Real-world experiments on fluid transfer, rubber band stretching, and whiteboard erasure demonstrate robust grasp stability and improved force modulation over vision-only and force-aware baselines.

Grasp State Estimation via 3D Mean Resultant Length

M. Suh, C. Mun, H. Lee, and J. Park

Proceedings of the Korea Robotics Society Annual Conference (KRoC), 2026.

This work proposes a grasp state estimation method based on cumulative tactile signal changes and a 3D extension of Mean Resultant Length. Instead of explicitly estimating contact force or friction, the method analyzes the directional concentration of tactile distributions to classify no-contact, grasp, and slip states. Experiments show that simple tactile distribution features can provide useful grasp-state feedback for adaptive manipulation.

SNU-Avatar Haptic Arm: A Hybrid QDD/DD Operator Interface for Teleoperation

E. Sung, S. You, J. Shin, S. Park, S. Hur, J. Kim, S. Byun, C. Mun, H. Lee, D. Kim, and J. Park

IEEE Access, vol. 14, pp. 57409-57426, 2026.

This paper introduces the SNU-Avatar Haptic Arm, a 6-DoF haptic operator interface designed for immersive humanoid robot teleoperation. The system combines quasi-direct-drive and direct-drive actuation to balance wide human-scale workspace, high backdrivability, and sufficient force output. Hardware analysis and experiments validate its force rendering capability, structural feasibility, and practical utility in teleoperation scenarios including the ANA Avatar XPRIZE Finals.
